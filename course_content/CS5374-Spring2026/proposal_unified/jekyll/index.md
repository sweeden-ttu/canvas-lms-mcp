# Trustworthy AI Legal and Governmental Content Validator

CS5374 Software Verification and Validation | Spring 2026

## Overview

This project builds a validation pipeline that verifies legal and governmental content against authoritative sources before AI systems surface it to users. Content types include legal news, judge names, elected officials, elections, laws, court documents, and templates.
